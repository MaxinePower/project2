Spoiled Eggs

"A rotten egg can't be spoiled"

We here at Spoiled Eggs believe that good movies are fine, but predictable. Why not sit back, relax and take in the worst of the worst. No expectations, just glorious, absolute trash. These films are low budget b films, lazily made parodies, and high budget flops. Get ready for bad acting, cringe, and some of the best unintentional comedies you'll come across.

Now pick your preferences and punish yourself!
